Who are we?

Trustline XRP was built for the millions of everyday people sending money across borders using XRPL, migrant workers sending money home, freelancers getting paid by foreign clients, small businesses paying overseas suppliers. People who can't afford to get it wrong.

Crypto is fast and cheap, but it comes with a problem nobody talks about: there's no way to know if the address you're sending to is a scammer, and no easy way to prove a payment happened after the fact. TrustCheck changes that. We scan any XRPL wallet address and tell you in plain English whether it looks safe or suspicious before you hit send. And once a payment is made, we turn that transaction into a clean, verifiable receipt that anyone, a landlord, employer, visa officer, or tax authority, can check in seconds.

No technical knowledge needed. Just the two things that matter most when real money is involved: trust before, proof after.
